🧹 Data Management
Your conversations are stored locally on your device only. We never share or sell your personal data to any third party. You have full control over your data at all times.
🔐 Security
All messages are encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption. Your conversations are private and protected. Feng AI does not store your messages on external servers.
📋 Usage Policy
Feng AI processes your messages using advanced AI technology to generate intelligent responses. Do not share sensitive personal information such as passwords, financial details, or ID numbers in chat.
👤 Your Rights
You have the right to delete all your data at any time. Clearing history removes all conversations permanently from your device. Feng AI respects your privacy and digital rights.
